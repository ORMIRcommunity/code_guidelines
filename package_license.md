## What license do I use for my code?
You can choose a permissive license (e.g. BDS, MIT, Apache) or a copyleft license (e.g. GNU GPL, Mozilla). If you are not familiar with open-source licenses, you can browse [choosealicense.com](https://choosealicense.com/licenses/), read the paper [A Quick Guide to Software Licensing for the Scientist-Programmer](https://journals.plos.org/ploscompbiol/article?id=10.1371/journal.pcbi.1002598), or watch [this video](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=GlAnKGBnhFY) (from minute 4)
